Pecan, raisin and cinnamon cookies

Prep time: 30 mins

Yield: 5 ½ dozen

INGREDIENTS










Steps

  1. Wash and dry lettuce and tear into bite-sized pieces or chop with a knife.
  2. Combine the 1/2 cup of oil, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 cup lemon juice, 1 whole egg, 1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce, and two cloves of garlic into a jar or dressing container and blend with a blender until smooth. Or, if you mince or press the garlic, simply shake the container until thoroughly mixed.
  3. Toss the lettuce with the dressing to desired amount of dressing, parmesan cheese, and croutons and season with fresh cracked black pepper to taste.
