Frozen Drinks

Prep time: 4 mins

Yield: 4

INGREDIENTS








Steps

  1. Chill two margarita glasses (or a really big margarita glass). Run the lime wedge around the rim of the chilled glasses and dip the rims in the coarse salt.
  2. In blender, combine the lime juice, grapefruit juice, hibiscus liqueur, orange liqueur, and silver tequila. Blend.
  3. Add ice and blend until smooth
  4. Pour into the prepared margarita glasses.
